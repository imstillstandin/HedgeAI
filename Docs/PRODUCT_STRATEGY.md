Market Opportunity
Foreign exchange markets process over $7 trillion per day in trading volume.
Small and medium-sized businesses account for a significant portion of international trade but typically lack tools to manage currency risk.
Research suggests:
80–95% of SMEs do not hedge FX exposure
many SMEs rely solely on spot conversions
FX risk is often treated as a normal operating cost
This creates a large opportunity for a simplified FX risk management platform.

Product Evolution Path
FX Risk Radar is expected to evolve through several stages.
Stage 1 — Exposure Analysis Tool
Core capabilities:
FX exposure detection
scenario analysis
hedge suggestion ranges
FX health score
Goal: validate the core value proposition.
Stage 2 — Continuous Monitoring System
New capabilities:
live FX monitoring
alert system
settlement warnings
market level alerts
Goal: become a tool SMEs leave running continuously.
Stage 3 — FX Intelligence Platform
New capabilities:
historical FX analysis
recurring exposure detection
exposure forecasting
currency concentration analysis
Goal: give SMEs treasury-level insights.
Stage 4 — Decision Support Platform
New capabilities:
strategy suggestions
hedging guidance
decision alerts
scenario planning
Goal: replicate the conversations experienced FX dealers have with clients.
Stage 5 — Execution Connectivity
Potential capabilities:
broker integration
trade request workflows
rate comparison
transaction routing
Goal: allow businesses to act directly from the platform.

Strategic Advantages
FX Risk Radar’s differentiation comes primarily from domain knowledge.
Key advantages include:
Behavioural insights
The platform incorporates real SME behaviour patterns observed in FX markets.
These insights influence:
alert timing
hedge suggestions
messaging style
Simplified risk framing
Instead of presenting market data, the platform translates FX movements into:
profit impact
cost impact
margin impact
Accessibility
Large corporations use treasury management systems.
SMEs typically cannot afford or operate such tools.
FX Risk Radar aims to provide treasury-level insights with SME simplicity.
Long-Term Vision
The long-term vision is for FX Risk Radar to become a treasury intelligence platform for SMEs engaged in international trade.
Possible future capabilities include:
automated FX exposure detection
currency risk monitoring
hedge strategy guidance
broker connectivity
cross-border payment insights
international trade analytics
Strategic Positioning
FX Risk Radar should not compete directly with:
FX brokers
banks
payment platforms
Instead, it should act as the decision intelligence layer that helps businesses manage currency risk before executing transactions.
Final Strategic Insight
Large companies already have treasury departments that monitor FX risk.
Small and medium-sized businesses typically do not.
FX Risk Radar aims to bridge this gap by providing automated FX risk intelligence tailored to SMEs.
